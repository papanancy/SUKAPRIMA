Instant Personal Loans: How Fast Can You Actually Get Funds?

Some loan providers offer instant loans, but processing times vary

With mostpersonal loans, it will take two to five days to get your money after your application is approved. Some lenders offer to approve your personal loan and send your money within the same working day when you apply.

However, a number of factors can delay the process. These include the time it takes for your personal loan lender to process your application, and to complete thecredit check. Even if your lender completes this step quickly, however, it might take a few days for your bank to process the payment and make it available for you.

KEY TAKEAWAYS

  • Some personal loan lenders process your loan application and send your money within the same day when you apply.
  • It can take a few days for these personal loan funds to become available in your bank account.
  • Research loan options before applying fora personal loan to get the best loan for you.
  • Personal loans generally offer better interest rates than other loans like credit cards and payday loans.

What Is an Instant Personal Loan?

An “instant” personal loan is apersonal loanin which the lender immediately makes a decision on whether to approve the loan. Theselendersaim to assess your application and send you your funds quickly.

An instant personal loan works in the same way as a standard personal loan. You can apply online for this type of loan, and your lender will perform acredit checkto determine if you qualify for the loan and whatinterest ratethey can offer you.

If you are approved for the loan and accept it, the lender will send you alump sumof money. You must then pay this money back, generally in equal monthly installments over a set period, which is typically a few months to several years. You’ll payintereston the loan, and sometimes fees.

Note

You can get a personal loan from financial institutions like banks, credit unions, and online lenders (which include loan apps).

How Fast Can You Get and Use a Personal Loan?

Some lenders offer fast processing times for personal loans. Among these lenders are:

  • Rocket Loans: With Rocket Loans, you can get up to $45,000 as soon as the same day you apply.1
  • LightStream: LightStream’s loans can be disbursed as soon as the same day you apply. Same-day funding is available if your application is approved, you sign the loan agreement, and you complete the verification process by 2:30 p.m. Eastern time and schedule funding by 3 p.m.2

However, while lenders may be able to process your loan application the same day you apply, two factors may slow down a personal loan application:

  • Finding a loan: Taking time to research lenders, interest rates, and theterms of your loanmay save you a significant amount of money in the long term.
  • Transfer times: Lenders will often send your money viaAutomated Clearing House (ACH) transfer. While this is potentiallyquicker than a wire transfer, it’s not instant. You’ll have to wait for your bank to accept the transfer and give you access to the funds, which can take a few days.

So, even if you are approved for a loan on the same day you apply, the process to receiving the money normally takes at least a few days.

Pros and Cons of Instant Personal Loans

As with any financial product, instant personal loans have pros and cons.

Pros

  • Fast funding

  • Cheaper than credit cards

  • Convenience

Cons

  • May require good credit

  • Fewer lenders to choose from

Pros Explained

  • Fast funding: Funds from your instant personal loan can be available within a day or two of applying for the loan, which may be helpful in anemergency.
  • Cheaper than credit cards: The interest rates on instant personal loans are generally much lower than those oncredit cards, which will save you money in the long term.
  • Convenience: You can research and apply for a personal loan online, which is more convenient than visiting a bank branch.

Cons Explained

  • May require good credit: Although many lenders say they can process your application within one working day, this generally applies to applicants withgood credit. If you havebad credit, your lender will typically look into your application in more detail, which can slow down your application.
  • Smaller choice of lenders: Only a few lenders offer same-day turnaround times for loans, so your choice of lenders is more limited if you want this kind of loan. You may have to either pay a higher interest rate or settle for loan terms that don’t fully meet your needs.

How to Get an Instant Personal Loan

The process of applying for an instant personal loan is largely the same asapplying for a regular personal loan. You should first determine how much you need. You can generally borrow $1,000 to $50,000 with a personal loan, though higher amounts are available that may require good credit and take longer to approve.

Then, review your finances to make sure you can comfortably make the monthly loanrepayments. Next, research andcompare lenders. You can use our review of thebest fast personal loansto compare prices and terms.

You’ll then want to try to pre-qualify for the loan if the lender offerspre-qualification. Most lenders will perform asoft credit checkto assess how much you can borrow and what interest rate they can offer.

Finally, you can apply online by filling out the lender’s application form. Generally, the applications are straightforward and require personal information like yourSocial Security numberandincome, among other data.

Alternatives to Instant Personal Loans

If you need cash quickly, an instant personal loan is often a good option, since these loans generally have lower interest rates than alternative sources. However, if you don’t want to use an instant personal loan or can’t qualify, you can consider other financing options. They include:

  • Cash-advance apps: These apps let you borrow money ahead of your next paycheck, and they can be available quickly. However,cash-advanceapps have higher interest rates than personal loans, especially if you’re not able to make payments on time.
  • Credit cards: Somecredit cardsoffer a 0% interest introductory period. If you can pay back your loan within this period, they can be a good deal. Make sure you pay back your balance before the offer ends, however, or you’ll have to pay the standard interest rate.
  • Payday loans:Payday loansalso provide money quickly, but they have high interest rates. Payday loans are also not available in all states.3
